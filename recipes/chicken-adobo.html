Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description


Chicken Adobo is definitely one of the classics that we have grown up with. Elders at our childhood home used to make this for us and they will sure earn a smile from us instantly because we absolutely loved this dish growing up! Who are we kidding—we love this dish even until now!


Made with braised chicken, mostly thighs and drumsticks, in a sauce that is a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorn, Chicken Adobo is definitely packed with flavor! Every mouthful of this dish is a delicious blend of tangy, salty, garlicky, and even sweet and spicy tones all thanks to the condiments and seasonings that make up a Chicken Adobo. On top of that, as we make sure to have the chicken slowly simmered in the sauce, we do end up with something that is rich and enjoyably tender. Yum, indeed!


And so, if you have been missing some homey Chicken Adobo, then why don’t you try following our recipe and delight in it in no time? You are not going to regret it, you can hold us to that!

Ingredients:


  • 1 kg chicken legs and thighs
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic head
  • 4 laurel
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tbsp peppercorn
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 cup water

Steps:


  1. Slice garlic and onion. Set them aside.
  2. Place chicken in a pan, then add laurel leaves, garlic, onion, peppercorn, salt, soy sauce, vinegar, and oyster sauce.
  3. Cover it with a lid and bring it to a boil.
  4. After a while, add water. Make sure the chicken is being cooked evenly.
  5. Cover again with a lid and let it simmer until chicken is tender.
  6. Separate chicken from the sauce.
  7. Preheat some oil in a new pan.
  8. Pan-fry the chicken.
  9. Remove the chicken from the pan after pan-frying it.
  10. Add the sauce back in the pan. Heat and stir it for a few seconds.
  11. Add the chicken onto it and let it sit there for a while.
  12. Transfer Chicken Adobo into a serving a dish. Serve and enjoy!
